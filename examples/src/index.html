Check tax on goods you bring into the UK

Use this service to:

  • check the customs, excise and VAT due on goods you bring into the UK
  • declare your goods and make a payment online up to 72 hours before you arrive back in the UK

To declare goods and pay taxes online you will need to know:

  • the exact price of your goods in the currency used
  • the quantity or volume of your goods
  • your passport number and payment card details
